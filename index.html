Wei Xue

 

Intro:

I am the Marie Curie Experienced Researcher in Imperial College London, working with Mike Brookes and Patrick Naylor since August 2015. I received my Ph.D degree from National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, in July 2015. Before that, I spent my college life during 2006~2010 in HuaZhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China, from where I obtained a Bachelor degree in Automation.My research interests are in source localization, speech enhancement, blind system identification, microphone arrays, and source separation. 

 

Research:

Speech and audio signal processing, with a focus on single/multi-channel speech enhancement, microphone array based sound source localization, blind system identification, and speech separation.
